About Uddin Consultants

Modern Structural Engineering Support for Homeowners

Uddin Consultants is a residential structural engineering consultancy focused on safe, practical design for home projects. We support homeowners with extension calculations, loft conversion structures, steel beam design, and alteration advice, with a clear emphasis on buildability and compliance.

Whether you are opening up your ground floor, extending at the rear, or reworking your roof space, our role is to provide dependable structural input that helps your project progress with confidence.
